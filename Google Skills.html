    Frequently asked questions

    How do I earn points?

    Points can be earned by doing a variety of activities that contribute to your learning. Such activities include taking a lab, passing a quiz, watching a video, completing a course, and more!
    What are leagues?

    Leagues are used to group learners into different tiers. There are currently four leagues: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Diamond. All learners begin in the Bronze League. Climbing to higher leagues will take commitment, hard work, and determination!
    How do leaderboards work?

    After earning some points, you will be placed into a new leaderboard with 29 other learners from your league, where you will compete to earn the most points. Leaderboards last for a week, starting from when they are first created. At the end of the week, the top 10 learners will earn a promotion to the next-highest league (if one exists), and the bottom 5 learners will be demoted down to the next-lowest league (if one exists). Only the points you earn during each week count towards that weeks leaderboard, with all learners starting at zero points when the leaderboard is first created.
    What happens if I take a break from learning?

    After your current leaderboard finishes, you will not be placed into a new leaderboard until you earn points again. So if you take a break from learning and therefore earn no points for a long period of time, you dont have to worry about being demoted during that time. Your league progress is safe!
    Is there a possibility that I could lose my points and league progress?

    This is an experimental feature, and so a hard reset of all learners leagues, points, and leaderboards may happen at any time during the experiment. These resets would only apply to points, leaderboards, and leagues - not any of your other data on the platform.
    How do other learners see me on their leaderboards?

    Other learners will see your selected avatar and an anonymized username on their leaderboards. They will not see your profile picture, nor your real name.
    How does my player name work?

    Your player name is generated randomly as a positive adjective followed by an animal in English, e.g. Glorious Koala. You cannot change your player name. Your player name is chosen once for each leaderboard. You will get a new player name for each leaderboard you participate in.
    Can I opt-out of this?

    Yes, you can opt-out of these features through your General account settings, and can freely opt back in at any time.
